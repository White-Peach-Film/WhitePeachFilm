WhitePeachFilm

Abstract

WhitePeachFilm is a decentralized platform designed to provide a secure, private, and decentralized way for users to access adult content. Built on blockchain technology, it prioritizes user privacy and community engagement. The platform is powered by the Witep Token, which enables various features such as revenue sharing, sponsorship, and platform governance.

Introduction

WhitePeachFilm aims to revolutionize the way adult content is accessed and consumed by leveraging blockchain technology. Our platform offers a secure and private environment for users, ensuring their data remains confidential and their interactions are anonymous.

Tokenomics

The Witep Token (WITEP) is the native cryptocurrency of WhitePeachFilm, built on the Tron blockchain. It serves multiple purposes within the ecosystem:

  • Funds platform development and operations.
  • Enables quarterly revenue sharing for top 100 holders.
  • Grants sponsorship status to top 10 holders, featured on the official website.
